Fathom Tools

Access network troubleshooting and monitoring tools via the Fathom menu at the toolbar (or via the Firefox menu on Android):

  • Pageload Performance displays the pageload time of the active tab.
  • Debug My Connection test your network configuration and connectivity if you are having problems with your network.
  • Home Network Discovery discover devices and network connectivity in your home network.
  • Monitoring Graphs explore browsing and network performance over time.

Pageload Performance

Fathom monitors the pageload times of the sites you visit (except when in private-browsing mode). The monitoring is light-weight and does not impact your network browsing. You can see the performance of current tab in the Fathom menu and over time statistics in the Monitoring Graphs tool. Optionally, you can contribute the collected data for our research.

Performance Monitoring

In addition to pageload times, Fathom monitors the performance of your system (CPU, load, memory) and network connectivity (signal quality on Wifi, cross-traffic and network delays) on the background. The monitoring is light-weight and does not impact your network browsing. You can browse the performance statistics using the Monitoring Graphs tool. Optionally, you can contribute the collected data for our research.

Data Collection Policy

Fathom project is a research project and our goal is to collect data on real home networks configuration, performance characteristics and typical problems via this tool. The extension will ask you for your permission to upload the results of the various network measurements to our secure server when you first use each tool. You can change the settings at anytime via the addon preferences (go to Menu -> Addons -> Fathom). Your data is stored on our secure servers and never shared with any 3rd parties.

In accordance of the French law on « informatique et libertés » of 6 January 1978 modified in 2004, you have the right to access and correct any personal information concerning you. You can request this right by sending us an email to fathom@inria.fr.
