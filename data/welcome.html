Fathom Tools

Fathom has a number of built-in tools for network monitoring and troubleshooting available in the Fathom Menu :

  • Pageload Performance displays the pageload time of the current active tab.
  • Debug My Connection test your network configuration and connectivity if you are having problems with your network.
  • Home Network Discovery discover devices and network connectivity in your home network.
  • Monitoring Graphs explore web browsing, network and system performance over time.

Contribute Data for Research

Fathom is a joint research project betweek MUSE team of Inria Paris-Rocquencourt (FR) and International Computer Science Institute (ICSI) at Berkeley (CA, US). We do research on on network quality of experience and root cause detection of performance degradations.

We use Fathom to collect data for our research. Data sharing is optional and you can use the Fathom extension and tools without contributing any data.

Next three slides present our privacy policy and collected data, and ask you for your data sharing preferences.

Pageload Performance

Fathom monitors on the background the pageload times of the sites you visit (except when in private-browsing mode). The monitoring is light-weight and does not impact your internet browsing. You can check the pageload performance of the current active tab in the Fathom Menu and historical data using the Monitoring Graphs tool.

If you choose to share data with us, the names of the sites you visit are anonymized except for a small number of popular whitelisted domains (such as google.com) that you can edit here. Any personally identifiable information (such as URL request parameters) are never recorded.

System Performance

Fathom monitors on the background the performance of your system (CPU, load, memory) and network connectivity (signal quality on Wifi, cross-traffic and network delays). The monitoring is light-weight and does not impact your internet browsing. You can explore the performance statistics using the Monitoring Graphs tool.
