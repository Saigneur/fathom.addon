Fathom Tools

Access network troubleshooting and monitoring tools via the Fathom Menu at the toolbar:

  • Pageload Performance displays the pageload time of the current active tab.
  • Debug My Connection test your network configuration and connectivity if you are having problems with your network.
  • Home Network Discovery discover devices and network connectivity in your home network.
  • Monitoring Graphs explore browsing and network performance over time.

Data Collection and Privacy

Fathom is a research project at MUSE team of Inria Paris-Rocquencourt. We do research on user experience and performance of home networks and internet applications. We use Fathom to collect data for this purpose. All data collection is optional and you can use the Fathom extension and tools without sharing any data.

Next two slides overview collected data and ask you for your data collection preferences. You can change these settings at anytime via the addon preferences page (Fathom Menu -> Preferences).

Pageload Performance

Fathom monitors on the background the pageload times of the sites you visit (except in private-browsing mode). The monitoring is light-weight and does not impact your network browsing. You can check the pageload performance of current active tab in the Fathom Menu .

Optionally, you can contribute pageload data for our research. The names of the sites you visit are anonymized except for a small number of popular whitelisted domains (such as google.com) that you can edit here.

Performance Monitoring

Fathom monitors on the background the performance of your system (CPU, load, memory) and network connectivity (signal quality on Wifi, cross-traffic and network delays). The monitoring is light-weight and does not impact your network browsing. You can explore the performance statistics using the Monitoring Graphs tool.
