What is This ?

Fathom monitors on the background the page load performance of websites you visit (excluding always anything you do in the private browsing mode). If allowed, we upload the performance data to our secure servers for network research purposes. By default, we anonymize the identity of the accessed domains. This means that we cannot know which pages or domains you visit or if you and any other Fathom user have visited the same domains.

However, to be able to correlate the network performance across users, we DO NOT anonymize the identify of a small set of popular domains listed below. You can remove any domains that you feel unconfortable about sharing with us by unchecking the names below (don't forget to save changes). The changes will only affect future data collection.
