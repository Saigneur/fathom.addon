What is This ?

Fathom monitors on the background the page load performance of websites you visit (excluding anything you do in the private browsing mode). If allowed, we upload the performance data to our secure servers for network research purposes. By default, we anonymize the identity of the accessed pages. This means that we cannot know which pages you visit or if you and any other user have visited the same pages.

However, to be able to correlate the network performance across users, we DO NOT anonymize the identify of a small set of popular hosts and pages listed below. You can remove any items that you do not wish us to know about by unchecking the names. The changes will only affect future visits.
