About Pageload Monitoring

Fathom monitors the pageload times of the sites you visit (except when in private-browsing mode). The monitoring is light-weight and does not impact your network browsing. You can see the performance of current tab in the Fathom menu and over time statistics in the Monitoring Graphs tool. Optionally, you can contribute the collected data for our research.

By default, we anonymize the identity of the accessed domains. This means that we cannot know which pages or domains you visit or if you and any other Fathom user have visited the same domains. In order to be able to correlate the network performance across users, we DO NOT anonymize the identify of a small set of popular domains listed below. You can remove any domains that you feel unconfortable about sharing with us by unchecking the names below (don't forget to save changes). The changes will only affect future data collection.
